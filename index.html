About Me (Duke Dantalion)

I am a Great and Mighty Duke, appearing in the form of a man with many countenances, all men's and women's faces. I have a book in my right hand.

My office is to teach all Arts and Sciences unto anyone and to declare the Secret Counsel of anyone; for I know the thoughts of all men and women and can change them at my will.

I can cause Love and show the likeness of any person anywhere in the world.

I govern 36 Legions of Spirits.

This is my sigil to be made in Copper

    Dantalion

    Dantalion is depicted as a handsome young man, often wearing regal robes and carrying a book. He is known to change his appearance based on the desires and thoughts of the person he is interacting with. Dantalion is a powerful demon, often associated with the teaching of knowledge and the arts. He is said to have the power to control the minds of others and is often invoked for the purpose of manipulation. He is also said to have the ability to cause one to fall into a deep sleep, from which they can be made to dream of anything the demon desires.
